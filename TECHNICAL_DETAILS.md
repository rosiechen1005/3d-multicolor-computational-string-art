# Technical details

This document will summarize the algorithms used in the extended string art pipeline (2D, multicolor, 3D). The base monocolor 2D formulation follows Birsak et al. (2018); our extensions add multicolor (automated palette selection, layered and unified optimization) and 3D (cubic pin frame, voxel targets, greedy L2 and Chamfer objectives).

**Full technical details—including objective functions, update rules, and complexity—will be provided in the accompanying paper upon publication.**
